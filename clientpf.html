Corporate Profile

[ Client Relations: Profile | Fact Sheet | Calendar of Events ]

AnciCare PPO is a leading preferred provider network focused on delivering superior medical imaging services to the workers compensation and automobile liability industries.  Through a nationwide network of independent diagnostic facilities, the Company's mission is to provide high quality, diagnostic services, including MRIs, CTs and Bone Scans, quickly and at preferred low costs.  “Our goal is to expedite the process so that doctors can develop a treatment plan and start the return to work as quickly as possible,” says Michael Cabrera, president of AnciCare.  “By managing the turnaround time, the benefits are twofold: productivity increases and indemnity costs decrease.”

AnciCare's market is the workers compensation system and the automobile liability industry, an increasingly expensive environment.  Each year, 3.9 million Americans receive some form of compensation due to vehicular accidents while an additional 7.5 million receive compensation due to workplace injuries.  In 1995, the bill for these services exceeded $80 billion in medical and direct services. The concept of managed care has provided a viable solution to help temper and contol this risk and preferred provider networks like AnciCare are an important part of this solution.  

The company distinguishes itself by selectively entering into relationships with facilities by offering more volume to those participating in the AnciCare network.  Because of the high volume of patients it refers to its affiliates, AnciCare is able to contract with its providers at competitive rates and pass those savings onto clients.  Additionally, AnciCare works with its clients to develop long-term strategic partnerships and design efficient, client-focused programs that effectively contain costs.  “Everyone benefits from the patient who gets well quicker, to the employer who gets their employee back to work sooner to the payer who’s job it is to facilitate the process.  It’s the perfect example of how managed care can be a win-win for all parties.”    

Another unique characteristic is that AnciCare only affiliates with independent facilities to ensure that there is no compromise as to the quality of care.  “We are extremely discerning and selective when it comes to the credential certification process.  All providers are board certified radiologists and the equipment must be state-of-the-art, the best the industry has to offer.  The environment must also be extremely service oriented, pleasant and patient-friendly,” said Cabrera.  

Founded in 1994 by Cabrera, the company has experienced controlled, steady growth and now has 700 facilities in 38 states and the District of Columbia and 1500 board certified radiologists.  In fact, the company services over 70% of the workers compensation industry.

Cabrera believes that Company's success is founded on its focused discipline and market niche.  "We are committed to being the very best in our field.  That means having a complete understanding of the challenges facing all parties involved in the claims process and delivering solutions that meet their needs and in a timeframe that makes sense.  We have developed a model for delivering imaging services that is preferred in every sense of the word.”  

For example, AnciCare's One Step Service Program is the hallmark of the company's client focused solutions.  One Step emphasizes expedient access to care so that the length of disability duration can be reduced.  The Program simplifies access to imaging services by: 

  • providing a phone-in or fax referral process

  • faxed appointment confirmation

  • appointments scheduled within 48 hours

  • medical records to adjuster, case manager and referring physician within 48 hours

  • 100% guarantee on the quality of film 

In addition, once the services are rendered, AnciCare further simplifies the invoicing process by providing all claims under one tax identification number so that facilities can submit bills directly to AnciCare reflecting the fee schedule or usual and customer price along with the negotiated rates. 

The trend in the healthcare industry has been one of more movement towards directing care independent of the line of coverage as a way to control rising medical costs.  AnciCare PPO has been leading this trend and setting the standard.  Says Cabrera, "When we first got into this business, the question was 'how can you do this?'  Now, the model is accepted and the question is, 'Can you do this for me?'"    

AnciCare’s customer base includes over 300 nationwide companies spanning multiple industry segments such as managed care organizations, insurance carriers, case management companies, third-party administrators and large employers.  Leading companies such as CNA, The Hartford, Kemper National Services, IntraCorp, Gallagher and Bassett, Corvel, RSKCo and CCN, rely upon AnciCare to help them manage the medical imaging component more efficiently by improving productivity, reducing indemnity and medical imaging costs, delivering greater functionality, and improving customer loyalty. AnciCare medical imaging services are available nationwide through a combination of direct sales force and managed care organizations.
