AnciCare PPO 
 

OVERVIEW

The goal of AnciCare's PPO is to significantly reduce costs by utilizing preferred medical imaging providers, thereby ensuring the highest quality of care at the lowest possible cost.  

At AnciCare, we believe that one of the best ways to ensure the cost-effective delivery of quality health care is to identify respected medical imaging providers who practice prudent health care management.  AnciCare has established contracts with such preferred providers throughout the nation.  These providers comprise our nationally recognized Medical Imaging PPO.

 

THE NATIONAL NETWORK

AnciCare's PPO is ranked as the best medical imaging network in the United States, making it one of the first of its kind.  Since its inception in 1994, AnciCare's PPO has grown to encompass 50 states across the nation, including Puerto Rico.  Our medical imaging network currently offers PPO coverage to thousands of clients and millions of individuals nationwide. 

AnciCare's comprehensive network is comprised of the nation's best radiologists and medical imaging facilities, all of whom meet AnciCare's standard of excellence.  Currently, AnciCare's PPO offers access to over 1,500 board certified radiologists and over 700 free-standing medical imaging centers nationwide.

  

NETWORK DEVELOPMENT

AnciCare's philosophy is to contract with medical imaging providers willing to comply with utilization review and quality assurance standards and meet other terms and conditions designed to provide, ultra-fast turnaround time and convenient patient access to high quality, cost effective care.

AnciCare has extensive knowledge and experience in contracting and negotiating network agreements with free-standing individual providers and facilities.  In addition, AnciCare has extensive experience in credentialing and network maintenance programs.     

 

CREDENTIALING

Our Network Development Team ensures that every medical imaging provider participating in our network meets our stringent credentialing criteria.  AnciCare's medical imaging providers were selected using a range of criteria which includes:  quality and reputation of facility, services provided, geographic distribution, adherance to utilization parameters, references/excellent reputation in community and adequate insurance coverage.  

Once a radiologist has been selected, they are invited to apply for participation in our Medical Imaging PPO network.  In order to participate, every radiologist must meet credentialing criteria such as board certified, current license, maintain adequate insurance coverage, and adhere to utilization review parameters and guidelines.

Verification of provider information is an ongoing effort at AnciCare.  Every participating medical imaging provider is required to uphold all elements of our standards and criteria, or automatic termination from the Network will occur.

 

EXPANSION OF THE NETWORKS

The development of our national Medical Imaging PPO is driven by the needs of our clients.  Our Network Development Team continually negotiates with medical imaging providers on behalf of our clients.  Network expansion may occur via two primary methods, Provider Nomination requests or general contracting.

Within the scope of our Provider Nomination Program, AnciCare welcomes the opportunity to contract medical imaging providers for participation on behalf of our clients.  When a client would like to nominate a provider, they need only submit a Provider Nomination to AnciCare's Network Development Team.  On behalf of the client, our Network Development Team contacts the provider and thoroughly evaluates the provider's credentials.  If the provider meets our stringent credentialing criteria and is not in direct competition with other participating providers, the provider is invited to participate in our Medical Imaging PPO Network.

 

CONTRACTS

AnciCare's PPO Provider Service Agreements are evergreen, renewing automatically on the original contract date.  At the point of renewal, each party has the opportunity to renegotiate the contract.  If at any point throughout the length of the contract a participating provider fails to meet our credentialing/recredentialing criteria, the provider is immediately terminated from our Medical Imaging Network.
