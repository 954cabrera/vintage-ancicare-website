Accounting

Accounts Payable

Immediate opening for an AP clerk in a fast paced office.  Must have general knowledge of accounts payable.  Candidate's responsibilities include the issuance of checks from our accounting system, update price files, research and resolve provider payment issues, and miscellaneous clerical responsibilities.

Administration
Administrative Assistant
Ideal candidate must be an exceptional executive assistant with strong PC skills, excellent grammar communication and organizational skills, and a minimum of 5 years experience reporting to a President/CEO of Senior Executive.  Bachelors degree required.
 

Receptionist

Responsibilities include general reception duties for AnciCare employees, visitors, applicants, vendors, etc. Provide administrative support to administration and assist with other general administrative work.  Track applicant Equal Opportunity Data for affirmative action reporting.  Must be proficient with MS WORD and be able to work with minimum supervision.  Position requires dependability, flexibility, a very positive attitude, and a professional demeanor and appearance.

Client Services Representative

Ideal candidate must have at least 2-3 years experience in customer service and demonstrate an excellent customer service attitude.  Excellent communication, PC and phone skills essential.  Very professional environment.  Only polished, professional individuals with strong communication need apply.  Bilingual preferred.  Full/Part-Time and varied shifts available between 7:00AM and 9:00 PM.  

 
Medical Records Representative
 
Responsible for the delivery of outstanding customer service in a fast paced environment where performance is based on excellence.  Will handle incoming and outgoing phone calls to clients, providers, patients, physicians and any other appropriate party deemed necessary.  These calls include, but are not limited to responding to inquiries with appropriate information verifying exam completion via fax and telephone (if required) within 24 hours scheduling at providers in no-show/cancelled appointment scenarios requesting reports from medical imaging facilities via fax and telephone (if required) within 48 hours after exam completion.
