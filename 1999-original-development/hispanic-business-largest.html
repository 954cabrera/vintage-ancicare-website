ANCICARE PPO RANKED AS ONE OF THE LARGEST AND FASTEST-GROWING HISPANIC-OWNED BUSINESSES IN THE COUNTRY

Hispanic Business Magazine Releases Rankings 

Miramar, FL – Managed Care Networks, Inc. d/b/a AnciCare PPO (AnciCare) has been ranked in two separate categories as one of the largest and fastest-growing Hispanic-owned businesses in the United States by Hispanic Business Magazine. 

In the first category, AnciCare was selected as one of the largest Hispanic-owned businesses in the United States and ranked 320 out of  500 in this years’ rankings by reporting in excess of $11 million in revenues.

In the second category, AnciCare was selected as one of the top 100 fastest-growing Hispanic-owned businesses in the United States and ranked 18th in this years' ranking by reporting a five year sales growth of 1,102% and a compound annual rate of 86%.

Michael Cabrera, Founder, President and CEO, stated “We share this tribute to our employees, our customers, and our medical providers that help make this happen for AnciCare.  The American dream is alive and well at AnciCare.  “AnciCare is the prototype company of the future:  a company where a diverse group of people, reflecting the make-up of this country, come together and work towards a common goal and succeed.”

AnciCare, founded in 1994, was formed as a specialty Preferred Provider Organization (PPO) to deliver superior medical imaging services, while assuring clients the benefits of efficient service and cost control. AnciCare offers a full range of medical imaging services including MRIs, CTs, and Bone Scans through its network of independent diagnostic facilities. These procedures are sold to managed care organizations, insurance companies, third party administrators, case management companies and employers.

For more information about Ancicare and its services, please contact Eileen Ramallo, Senior Vice President and COO, AnciCare PPO, 3700 Lakeside Drive, Suite 401, Miramar, FL  33027, 1-800-414-4MRI (4674).
