Company Overview

 

Bridging The Need

We Create Win-Win Relationships By Delivering Client Service And Satisfaction  

AnciCare is the Preferred Provider Network of Medical Imaging Services that delivers “Preferred Treatment” in every sense of the term. At AnciCare, everything is preferred, from our certified medical imaging facilities and superior quality images, to our unmatched professionalism. The full service of AnciCare assures preferred low pricing and guaranteed service.

At AnciCare, we build relationship by building trust. And we’ve grown to become one of the largest medical imaging PPOs in the United States. Annually, we serve over 70 % of the Workers’ Compensation Industry in our ever-expanding marketplace. To the benefit of our clients our prices are established at an average of 50% of the Workers’ Compensation Fee Schedule. All patient appointments are scheduled within two to three working days. And, of course, AnciCare accommodates “stat” patients immediately.

Our technology, our level of service and our professionals are unmatched in the industry. We are dedicated to providing superior quality managed medical imaging programs that answer our clients’ needs. By combining these considerable strengths with excellence in medical standards and business systems, we are able to provide our clients with effective, efficient, cost-containment programs.

We are pleased to serve major managed care organizations, insurance companies, third party administrators, case management companies, employers and other professionals throughout the United States. Our clients depend on AnciCare to provide the finest quality medical imaging product, time-efficient patient service and preferred low cost….and we deliver!

Let the managed care professionals at AnciCare show you the proactive way to preferred certified medical imaging facilities, quality images, medical and technical professionalism, low pricing and guaranteed service. You’ll like the way we mind your business.
