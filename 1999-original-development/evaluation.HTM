Evalution Criteria's

Both free standing and hospital-based providers are evaluated with regard to: 

  • levels of professional clinical staff;

  • degree of physician involvement with patients;

  • overall staffing levels;

  • education and experience requirements for the clinical staff including credentials and treatment responsibilities;

  • clinical staff privileging mechanisms;

  • frequencies and types of clinical supervision provided;

  • design and functional level of existing quality assurance and medical peer review programs;

  • responsiveness to consumers/payers;

  • Medical Director presence and responsibilities;

  • facility commitment to in-service training and continuing education; and

  • quality and frequency of calibration of sensitive equipment.

In our credentialing process, we specifically require response capabilities and appointment availability of 72 hours and accommodation of “stat” patients.
