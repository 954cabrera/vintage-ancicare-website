Scheduling Procedures

 

·        AnciCare is responsible for all contacts with all Payors related to the Agreement. Only AnciCare employees are authorized to contact its Payors/clients. 

·        AnciCare  coordinates and schedules all patients to authorized diagnostic imaging centers that participate in the AnciCare  PPO network. 

·        Provider will schedule Covered Persons for Covered Services to be rendered within  seventy-two (72) hours of receipt of the request for said services from AnciCare. 

·        Covered Persons identified by AnciCare or the referring physician as STAT patients must be seen on the same business day, if scheduling permits, but no later than twenty four (24) hours of receipt of the request for said services from AnciCare. 

·        AnciCare Case Coordinators will provide the Provider with an eleven (11) digit alphanumeric code which authorizes payment only for the Covered Services being scheduled. Authorizations are specific to the location, type and volume of service as well as specific to the equipment requested by AnciCare. Example: If Provider has two or more types of magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) machines at the requested location, AnciCare will specify and arrange payment to the Provider for the machine requested i.e., Provider is not allowed to reschedule a Covered Person from a designated and authorized closed bore MRI to an open bore MRI or vice versa. 

·        Provider is required to contact the referring physician to verify the specific physician orders (prescription) for the procedure(s) to be performed. If Provider fails to verify physician orders, the Payor or AnciCare will not be liable for the payment of service(s). If the physician requests procedures which differ from or are in addition to the specific procedure(s) contained in the original referral authorization from AnciCare, Provider shall contact AnciCare immediately to request a revision to the authorization. If Provider fails to notify AnciCare of the aforementioned discrepancy, the Payor or AnciCare will not be liable for any of the services rendered. 

·        Provider can not render service without copy of script from referring physician. 

·        If a referring physician attempts to schedule an AnciCare Covered Person directly with Providers location, Provider is required to call AnciCare for authorization.  All claims pertaining to any AnciCare Covered Person referred to Providers location must be submitted directly to AnciCare. 

·        Provider is prohibited from allowing Referring Physicians to read/interpret patients film/scan.  AnciCare  will only accept reading/interpretation from Board Certified Radiologists or a Medical Doctor. 

·        If any changes are made to the Covered Persons appointment (i.e. change of time, no shows, re-scheduling, additional services, etc.), AnciCare must be contacted as soon as any changes are made.  If an AnciCare Covered Person requires additional services, Provider is required to obtain authorization prior to rendering services by contacting the Customer Service Department at (800) 414-4674. Otherwise provider will not be paid for non-authorized services. 

·        If, for whatever reason, Provider is unable to accommodate a previously scheduled AnciCare referred Covered Person, Provider is required to notify AnciCare immediately by contacting the Customer Service Department at (800) 414-4674. Provider is prohibited from referring the Covered Person to another facility for Covered Services, even if the other facility is a Participating Provider within the AnciCare PPO network. 

·        Provider will not be paid if the patient

-         Did not show up for the appointment

-         Was late

-         Incomplete studies

-         For any unused dosage 

For any of the above mentioned, Provider is prohibited from billing payor, AnciCare or the patient. 

·        Provider will not charge payor, AnciCare or the patient for the cost of the first set of films. 

·        AnciCare must receive the medical imaging report within twenty-four (24) hours after services have been rendered.  Reports are to be faxed to AnciCare directly; NO COVER PAGE is necessary. AnciCares fax number is:

(954) 441-6350.

 

·        All claims are to be mailed to:

      AnciCare PPO                                                              ClaimsDepartment                                                                       3700 Lakeside Drive                                                            Suite 401                                                                       Miramar, FL 33027-3225 

·          Claims must be billed to AnciCare at the Providers Usual and Customary Rates (UCR) and must have the Providers staff or contracted radiologists signature and license number along with a copy of the radiologists medical imaging report. AnciCare  will not accept or be responsible for any claims submitted by a radiologist or other physician that has not been fully credentialled by AnciCare as a Participating Provider.

 

·          In states where there is  no workers compensation fee schedule and/or there is no workers compensation fee for a particular service  provider will  reimbursed at usual and customary rates as determined by the payors.  Standard for UCR, which is defined as per HIAA-Health Insurance Association of America, or as determined by their protocols.
