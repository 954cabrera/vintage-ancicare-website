Auto Managed Care Solutions

As premiums for auto insurance policies skyrocket out of control, the casualty industry and policy owners are seeking innovative alternatives that help curtail the escalation of unnecessary costs.  One of the most innovative strategies gaining momentum in these casualty markets is the incorporation of managed care programs that actively manage an accident from the initial episode throughout the course of a claim.   

Our specialty medical imaging network for auto casualty provide the benefits of credentialed provider networks, convenient scheduling of medical imaging diagnostic studies and drastic reductions in the costs of medical imaging services. Further, as medical costs are a factor in claims negotiations, reductions in medical costs can have significant impacts on the total settlement exposure.   

AnciCare has established a network of credentialed medical imaging centers in over 38 states that is currently available to provide the benefits of our preferred rates for policy holders who are channeled to the AnciCare network.  By utilizing our medical imaging network you automatically gain access to medical imaging services such as MRIs at discounted rates. The process for scheduling the policy holder will be the same as that for scheduling a workers’ compensation claimant.    

AnciCare has gone a step further in introducing auto claim handling in the State of Florida.  AnciCare recognizes that the State of Florida is poised to initiate managed care for policy holders within the next two to three years.  In preparation of anticipated legislation, AnciCare providers in the state of Florida have already agreed to allow AnciCare to re-price auto bills to our discounted rates, thereby allowing AnciCare to offer our discounts to clients for auto policy holders who are not directed to our centers by managed care.  Our medical imaging services and discounts are available now for immediate and dramatic impact on savings.   

In order to provide you company with our preferred rates for policy holders, AnciCare must be assured that payment will be made.  In other words, AnciCare requires authorization for payment in full of the AnciCare preferred rate.  AnciCare is not in a position to obtain payment from auto policyholders that may not have exhausted their Personal Injury Protection or deductible.

In all other states, AnciCare can provide your company access to the network for auto liability claims when patients request information on available imaging  facilities.
