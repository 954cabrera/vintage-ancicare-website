Automated Bill Review

 

Utilizing sophisticated software and a data base management approach, AnciCare’s automated bill review system screens bills for medical services to prevent over itemization of charges and re-prices bills to our negotiated rates.  

Bills are forwarded from the provider directly to the AnciCare claims unit who reviews the bill against pre-certification recommendations, verifies dates of service, and processes the bill.  The patient file is located within our custom designed scheduling and billing system known as CDL.  Once the patient is located in CDL, the patient information is verified against social security number, date of birth, date of injury, claim number, patient’s address, referring physician, and employer.   

Utilization Review is performed on each procedure submitted by the provider to verify authorization of the procedure and the relationship of the CPT 4 code to the ICD 9 code. Verification of the provider status in the network is made to verify that the date of service coincides with our records.   

When an unauthorized procedure is billed, AnciCare will contact you and request authorization.  In the event the procedure is not authorized, AnciCare will deny payment to the medical imaging facility.  AnciCare will not bill you for unauthorized procedures.   

CDL will automatically generate the usual and customary rate, the PPO contracted rate, and the savings generated.  The claim is then attached to the medical imaging report and sent to the appropriate insurance payor.  This is in addition to faxing the medical imaging report directly to the appropriate claims adjuster and case manager within 48 hours of the study.
