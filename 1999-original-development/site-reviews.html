Site Reviews

We conduct reviews designed to allow AnciCare to assess a potential provider facility as part of the credentialing process.  Areas of discussion with key personnel include: 

  • facilities’ knowledge and attitude towards managed care and their willingness to communicate with AnciCare Case Coordinators;

  • for in-patient facilities, current accreditation status with JCAHO, what focus visits were required, and the facilities status as to correcting any deficiencies cited;

  • detailed discussion of their quality improvement program, including ongoing monitors, assessments of patient outcomes and specific studies, focusing on medical staff peer review functions and equipment calibration;

  • risk management program, incident reporting, trending of incidents and how that information is used to reduce exposure;

  • method of reviewing X-ray results including the discrepancy rate, as measured in the quality improvement program;

  • process of the center’s credentialing of their clinical staff;

  • use of patient satisfaction surveys;

  • policies regarding calibration of sensitive equipment throughout the center; and

  • display of fire evacuation plan and frequency of drills.
