STRATEGIC PARTNERSHIPS

Our network providers are recruited for their reputation the healthcare community, and their willingness to participate in managed care programs.  AnciCare is a national medical imaging preferred provider organization specializing in ancillary services and cost containment programs for workers' compensation, group health and auto managed care.   

Our clients include workers’ compensation insurance carriers, case management companies, third-party administrators, self-insured employers and managed care organizations.  Our PPO produces tangible savings for our clients through our medical management programs, and more importantly the quality of services and savings our contracted providers offer our clients.  

It is our goal and philosophy to make this a “win win”  relationship for our network, our clients and our providers.   We offer our providers significant referral volume, and to accomplish this we will limit the participation of other medical imaging facilities with similar equipment and modality within the same community.  

AnciCare is actively involved in our clients' commitment to promote network utilization.  Aggressive channeling mechanisms are employed to maximize the directed use of network providers.
