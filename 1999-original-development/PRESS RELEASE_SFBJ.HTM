FEBRUARY 5, 1999

Medical Imaging PPO with a specialty niche work its way to top of list.

South Florida Business Journal Releases Rankings 

Miramar, FL – Managed Care Networks, Inc. d/b/a AnciCare PPO (AnciCare) has been ranked as one of the largest Preferred Provider Organization in South Florida.

AnciCare ascended to the number one position in this years' rankings by reporting the number of South Florida subscibers in excess of 500,000 up from 491,000 last year.

Michael Cabrera, Founder, President and CEO, stated "Specialty networks are a niche that is fairly new.  We fill a need for medical imaging because we deliver a high level of service, a huge variation of products and because we have the most accommodating facilities.  We offer the Mercedes of MRIs."

AnciCare, founded in 1994, was formed as a specialty Preferred Provider Organization (PPO) to deliver superior medical imaging services, while assuring clients the benefits of efficient service and cost control. AnciCare offers a full range of medical imaging services including MRIs, CTs, and Bone Scans through its network of independent diagnostic facilities. These procedures are sold to managed care organizations, insurance companies, third party administrators, case management companies and employers.
