Guarantee of Service

 

There are two important components to the services rendered by AnciCare; the professional and the technical.  By combining the best of both, our goal is to provide the highest level of client and patient service.  We have established a “complaint-free mission” and achieve that goal by providing qualified professionals, assuring state-of-the-art equipment and settling for nothing less than the best service to our clients and their claimants.   

An example: Should a referring physician question the medical imaging study results or reading, AnciCare will send the film to an independent Board Certified Radiologist and obtain an independent, objective reading and second opinion on the quality of the films, at no charge.  In most cases the original and second opinion are consistent.  The independent report is then sent to the referring physician and the adjuster and case manager overnight.  This colleague-to-colleague service assures that no time is lost and is unique to AnciCare.  In the event the second reading supports the need for a repeat study, AnciCare will schedule the second medical imaging study at no charge.   

Another example: We have another unique policy at AnciCare -- No Charge for No Shows.  And there is no charge if full patient service has not been rendered.  In addition, there is no charge for duplicate film requests on the part of the claims adjuster or case manager.  Our policies are exceptional in the industry.   

And yet another example: To assure the best service to patients, should the examinee have difficulty getting to and from their AnciCare appointment, we will arrange transportation.  All the client has to do is originate the request, and we will take care of the scheduling of transportation.   

It is this commitment to our product and service that establishes AnciCare as the “preferred” provider.   

AnciCare’s intellectual capital and industry resources provide your clients an opportunity to utilize a national medical imaging network that will deliver the highest quality imaging services and excellent marks in client satisfaction.  AnciCare is committed to working with your Company to provide the most comprehensive workers’ compensation and auto liability cost containment system possible for medical imaging.  In addition, AnciCare will provide your Company with a guarantee of service that is unparalleled in the industry.
