Performance Reporting

 

AnciCare realizes that your Company’s obligation to clients includes proof of cost containment.  AnciCare’s ad hoc report writer allows us to create management reports for your Company that show savings per case manager and claims adjuster and per regional office location.  Definitions of savings will be confirmed and reports demonstrating these savings will be generated in order to ensure your Company gets accurate information concerning AnciCare program savings.   

Reports will be furnished monthly, quarterly, and annually.  The reports will summarize the activity of all case managers as they relate to referring claimants to AnciCare. The reports include the amount that would be paid under workers’ compensation reimbursement rates, the amount paid under the AnciCare program, and the percentage of savings realized.
