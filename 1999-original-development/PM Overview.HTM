Overview of AnciCare PPO

AnciCare PPO, a division of Managed Care Networks, Inc., is one of the largest medical imaging PPO networks in the United States specializing in ancillary services and cost containment programs for workers’ compensation, group health and auto medical injuries and illness.   Operating since 1994, we service many of the largest managed care organizations, insurance companies, case management organizations, third party administrators, and self-insured employers in the nation.   We understand the business community’s needs for quality, cost effective diagnostic health care while assuring our clients the benefits of efficient service and cost control. 

Our goal is to provide our clients with the most comprehensive and easily accessible diagnostic healthcare possible.  It is with this in mind that AnciCare continues to expand our network throughout the United States.  Our clients are encouraged to request specific expansion areas and are given immediate attention by our Network Development team.  AnciCare’s present network includes over 500 diagnostic facilities throughout 29 states. 

All facilities are independently owned and operated.  Neither AnciCare nor the referring physicians own or have a vested interest in the participating facilities. 

To ensure the highest quality of services, participating facilities’ credentials are reviewed based upon guidelines set forth by the National Committee of Quality Assurance (NCQA).  AnciCare verifies all board certified credentials, state licenses, DEA certificates, and professional liability coverage.  We qualify the technology and diagnostic environment. It is important that participating facilities utilize state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment and provide a patient-friendly environment. 

By integrating professionalism and technology with the highest quality medical standards and business systems, AnciCare is able to offer and service efficient programs that effectively increase referral volume. 

At AnciCare, we are committed to providing superior quality managed healthcare programs that are responsive to our clients’ needs and dedicated to their satisfaction.
