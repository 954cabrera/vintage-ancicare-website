Medical Imaging Solutions

AnciCare PPO was formed in 1994 to serve as a specialty preferred provider organization (PPO) to deliver superior medical imaging services nationwide, while assuring clients the benefits of efficient service and cost control.  AnciCare offers a full range of imaging including magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), computed axial tomography (CAT Scan) and nuclear medicine (Bone Scan) among other modalities through a contracted network of independent diagnostic imaging facilities.  AnciCare's procedures are sold to managed care organizations (MCOs), insurance carriers, case management companies, third-party administrators (TPAs) and large employers.  AnciCare has focused on providing its services to the workers' compensation industry but also accepts referrals from the auto casualty and group health industries.

AnciCare's reputation for delivering high quality ultra-fast service is based on our unique "One Step Service" program, which encompasses the following:

  • Coordination of the diagnostic imaging procedure
  • Coordination of the related medical records
  • Administration of the claim for AnciCare's customers

Once a patient is referred, AnciCare will schedule the procedure, notify the patient, case manager, adjuster and physician of the appointment, ensure the completion of the procedure and distribute the medical report.  AnciCare invoices the claim reflecting its preferred rates.  

At AnciCare, We believe that our strategies complement the managed care environment associated with workers' compensation as well as auto casualty and group health by providing high quality service with contained costs.  AnciCare's "One Step Service" shifts responsibilities from case managers and adjusters to AnciCare, thereby increasing the productivity of our customers.
