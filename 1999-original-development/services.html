Services

Case Coordination
At AnciCare, our client service associates will coordinate the injured workers' examination within 48 hours and provide you with the medical report within 24 hours of receipt.

Automated Bill Review
AnciCare will process provider bills, conduct appropriate utilization review, and supply you with a utilization report indicating the usual and customary rate, the contracted rate, and the savings generated for each procedure authorized.

Performance Reporting
AnciCare's sophisticated PPO Information System is able to produce region specific and aggregate management reports for you.  Our custom designed information system allows claims administrators and case managers to share savings reports with clients.  Our succinct, timely and accurate reports ease the burden of claims administration and ensure leveraged decision-making.

Quality Improvement Program
AnciCare is dedicated to continuous quality management and improvement.  Our Quality Improvement program focuses not only on provider performance, but strongly emphasizes customer service.

Guarantee of Service
AnciCare guarantees the quality of our products, professionals, policies and procedures to provide you the most accommodating, most attentive service possible.  Our pricing is all inclusive.  There are no hidden costs for duplicate films, no shows, or second opinions.  This is unique to AnciCare in the industry.
