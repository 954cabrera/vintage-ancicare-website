AnciCare PPO is a leading service provider of medical imaging solutions, which includes a network of 700 free-standing and hospital based facilities providing Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), Computerized Axial Tomography (CAT Scan) and Nuclear Medicine (Bone Scan).

Michael Cabrera, President, Chief Executive Officer and Co-founder

Michael Cabrera is president, chief executive officer and co-founder of AnciCare PPO Inc.  Mr. Cabrera is a leading visionary and a pioneer in implementing insurance radiology solutions for workers’ compensation and auto insurance marketplaces.  He co-founded AnciCare in 1994 with a simple vision:  to deliver superior nationwide medical imaging services, while assuring clients the benefits of efficient service and cost control.   Mr. Cabrera’s vision, passion, and energy has led AnciCare to become the leading medical imaging network in the United States.  As CEO, Mr. Cabrera is responsible for planning, communicating and managing AnciCare’s business decisions and policies. 

Prior to co-founding AnciCare, Mr. Cabrera was director of managed care at International Magnetic Imaging Inc., where the company achieved record levels of managed care sales while increasing the company’s managed care revenues by over 200% annually.  Mr. Cabrera also held management positions at Healthcare Support Group and Mercy Hospital. 

Mr. Cabrera holds a B.B.A degree from Florida International University and an M.B.A. degree from Nova University. 

 

Donna Cabrera, Co-founder

As co-founder and Sr. Vice President of Quality Assurance, Donna Cabrera is responsible for the quality, training  and service of our delivery system.

Ms. Cabrera created the company's customer service program to meet the needs of our clients.  She has created numerous programs, including the Company's hallmark "One Step Service" which impacts the Company's ability to deliver ultra-fast client services and efficiencies.

 

 

Eileen Ramallo, Senior Vice President and Chief Operating Officer

As Senior Vice President and COO, Eileen Ramallo is responsible for operational oversight along with sales and marketing efforts for promotion of AnciCare PPO services to property & casualty insurance carriers, third party administrators, managed care organizations and case management companies.  

Prior to joining AnciCare, Ms. Ramallo served as Vice President, Cost Management for RSKCo/CNA Insurance from 1997 to 1999. In this position, Ms. Ramallo was responsible for development, marketing and implementation of integrated disability management programs for CNA Risk Management in partnership with CNA’s Special Benefits operation. Before joining RSKCo, Ms. Ramallo served in managed care services with KemperCare-24, Kemper Insurance Company, in Long Grove, Il., from 1995 to 1997. She was with Kemper National Services in Plantation, Fl., from 1988 to 1995, serving as vice president disability case management, from 1994 to 1995; assistant vice president, health care coordination, from 1992 to 1994; branch manager, field case management (Sacramento, California), from 1991 to 1992; corporate services manager from 1990 to 1991; and medical/vocational case manager from 1988 to 1990. 

Ms. Ramallo has earned the designations of Certified Insurance Rehabilitation Specialist and Certified Case Manager. She has participated as guest speaker in numerous conferences on integrated disability management and is featured in Who’s Who of America’s Business Women.   

Ms. Ramallo received a Bachelor of Science degree in psychology from Florida International University in Miami and a Master of Science degree in mental health consulting from Barry University in Miami Shores, Fla.

Fred Steinberg, Medical Director

Dr. Fred Steinberg, practicing radiologist in Palm Beach, Florida, is the Medical Director of AnciCare PPO. In this position, Dr. Steinberg is responsible for medical leadership and oversight of selection and credentialing for participating medical imaging facilities.  Presently, Dr. Steinberg holds two university faculty positions: Clinical Assistant and Professor at the University of Miami School of Medicine, a position he has held since 1995; and Research Professor at Florida Atlantic University College of Science, a position he's held since 1998. In addition, Dr. Steinberg has served as an MRI consultant to the Expert Radiology in Beverly Hill, CA.  Dr. Steinberg came to AnciCare from MRI of Boca Raton where he was the Director of MRI Services from September 1992 to June 1998. One year prior, he was Director of the Center for Vascular and Advanced Body MRI Roxsan Radiology, Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles.   

Dr. Steinberg received his M.D. degree from Northwestern University Medical School, Chicago, IL and his B.S. degree from Northwestern University in Evanston, IL. He completed his residency at the University of Chicago Hospital and received post-doctoral training in Vascular Radiology at Massachusetts General Hospital and Magnetic Resonance Imaging at Northwestern Medical School. He also received his M.S. Degree from the University of California.  Dr. Steinberg is professionally affiliated with numerous associations such as the Radiological Society of North America (RSNA), American Roentgen Ray Society, Society of Magnetic Resonance, American Heart Association Council on Cardiac Radiology, International MR Angio Club and the American College of Radiology.

George Tayar, Controller

As Controller for AnciCare PPO, Mr. Tayar is responsible for overseeing all aspects of accounting, financial reporting and management information systems for the company.  

Prior to joining AnciCare, Mr. Tayar was assistant controller at All American Semiconductor, Inc., the 16th largest distributor of electronic components in the United States.  Previous to All American, George worked for Alamo Rent A Car, the 4th largest car rental company in the United States.   George is a Certified Public Accountant and an Associate in Risk Management.  George was recognized for his assistance with Management Accounting Guideline # 49 Adopting and Implementing Shared Services.  George is also an adjunct professor of Accounting at Florida Metropolitan University.  

George holds his Masters degree in accounting from Florida Atlantic University and his Bachelors degree, also in accounting, from New York University.  George is currently pursuing a second Bachelors degree in Computer Information Systems.
