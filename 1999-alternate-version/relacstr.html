ANCICARE PPO STRENGTHENS MANAGEMENT TEAM            

MIRAMAR, FL, October 4, 1999 – In a move to significantly strengthen its corporate management team, AnciCare PPO, a leading preferred provider network of medical imaging services, announced today the appointments of two new executives.  Joining the company are Eileen F. Ramallo as Senior Vice President and COO and Fred L. Steinberg, M.D., as Medical Director.

“It gives me great pleasure to welcome Eileen and Fred to our rapidly growing company,” said Michael Cabrera, President and CEO of AnciCare PPO. “They bring a vast wealth of knowledge, complete understanding of the industry, tremendous skills and a natural talent for leadership and innovation that are truly impressive.  They are the right people to have at the helm as AnciCare continues to pursue its mission to be the very best preferred provider of medical imaging services in the marketplace.” 

Eileen Ramallo comes to AnciCare with over 10 years of expertise in the casualty insurance industry.  In her current position, Ramallo is responsible for operational oversight along with sales and marketing efforts.  Before joining AnciCare, Ramallo spent two years at RSKCo/CNA Insurance where she served as vice president of Cost Management.  Ramallo was responsible for the development, marketing and implementation of integrated disability management programs for CNA Risk Management in partnership with CNA’s Special Benefits operation.  Prior to RSKCo/CNA Insurance, Ramallo worked for two years as managed care services officer with KemperCare-24, Kemper Insurance company.  Before KemperCare-24, Ramallo spent seven years with Kemper National Services in Plantation, Florida where she worked her way up from medical/vocational case manager to vice president disability case management.

Dr. Fred Steinberg joins AnciCare as Medical Director.  In this position, his responsibilities include medical leadership and oversight of imaging facility selection and credentialing.

Steinberg received his medical degree from Northwestern Medical School in 1982 and has since completed two fellowships in Vascular Radiology and Magnetic Resonance Imaging.  His experience in Radiology started as an Assistant in Radiology at the Massachusetts General Hospital at Harvard Medical School to Director  of Cardiac, Vascular and Body MRI Department of Radiology at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center in Los Angeles, California.  After Cedars-Sinai, Steinberg served as the Director for the Center for Vascular and Advanced Body MRI Roxsan Radiology in Beverly Hills before moving to his current title as a MRI Consultant for the Expert Radiology Network Boca Raton, Florida.  Steinberg is also a practicing radiologist at Westside Regional Medical Center in Plantation, FL.

About AnciCare PPO

Founded in 1994, AnciCare PPO (www.ancicare.com) is a leading preferred provider network focused on delivering superior medical imaging services to the workers compensation and automobile liability industries.  AnciCare’s network includes 700 facilities in 38 states and the District of Columbia and 1500 board certified radiologists.  

Based in Miramar, FL, AnciCare is a division of Managed Care Networks, Inc.
