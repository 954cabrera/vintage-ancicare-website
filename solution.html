Solutions

Medical Imaging Solutions
AnciCare delivers the quality, manageability, and innovation you expect from a nationwide market leader of medical imaging solutions.  From Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) to Computerized Axial Tomography (CAT Scan) to Nuclear Medicine (Bone Scan), AnciCare offers you a portfolio of medical imaging solutions that help sharpen your competitive edge.  Our customers include managed care organizations, insurance carriers, case management companies, third-party administrators and large employers.  

Pre-Certification Solutions
AnciCare pioneered the concept of the pre-certification of medical imaging services to provide more effective and more efficient healthcare to our customers.  AnciCare will work with customers to design a tailored program for the review of MRI and other costly diagnostic procedures.

Second Opinion Solutions
AnciCare offers Second Opinion Solutions that allow for the reading of any questioned magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), computerized axial tomography (CAT Scan) or Nuclear Medicine (Bone Scan).  All questioned studies are interpreted by board-certified radiologists.

Auto Managed Care Solutions
AnciCare's specialty medical imaging network for auto casualty provide the benefits of credentialed provider networks, convenient scheduling of medical imaging diagnostic studies and drastic reductions in the costs of medical imaging services.
