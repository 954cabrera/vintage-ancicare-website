Pre-Certification Solutions

As AnciCare continues to look for ways to bring value-added services to its’ clients, we have focused on finding methods to further reduce the lag time between request for imaging services and actual delivery of such. AnciCare’s Pre-Certification service is geared to expedite the authorization of request for diagnostic services when a question regarding medical necessity and appropriateness arises. 

Utilizing the QualityFIRST software program, in conjunction with experienced case managers, we prospectively review the request utilizing the decision support tool and evaluate the appropriateness of the planned health care for the patient. Once the referral for pre-certification services has been made, AnciCare will deliver a review within 4 hours.  If the provider’s proposed plan match the QualityFIRST guideline, the reviewing case manager closes the case. If there are variances, the reviewer first notifies the referring adjuster or case manager, and then refers to a specialist for a Peer to Peer consultation focused on resolving the variances and making final recommendations for treatment and resource use.  

By significantly reducing the turn around time for pre-certification services along with prompt scheduling of medically appropriate services, AnciCare can provide your Company with an outsource solution to better meet your client’s needs.
