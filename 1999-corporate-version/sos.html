Second Opinion Solutions

AnciCare has implemented a second opinion program for all clients that allow for the reading of any questioned MRI, CT or Bone Scan by a second board certified radiologist.  When this reading is requested in connection with a medical imaging procedure that was scheduled by AnciCare at a contracted AnciCare facility, this second opinion is obtained free of charge, as a part of the AnciCare Guarantee of Service. 

AnciCare now offers your company a Second Opinion Program for confirmation of diagnosis resulting from medical imaging examinations that were not scheduled by AnciCare at an AnciCare network provider location.   

In the event your company believes a second opinion is required for an MRI, CT or Bone Scan, the adjuster or case manager can call our client services department to request a second opinion.  A client service representative will ask for the patient’s name, the name and phone number of the center who conducted the examination, and the adjuster’s name and phone number. 

AnciCare will contact the center that performed the medical imaging examination and request that the films be sent to an AnciCare board certified radiologists for review. 

In some cases, a release of responsibility may be requested by the MRI center that conducted the study.  If so, AnciCare will contact your company to arrange that a release be sent via fax to the MRI center.  Once release is obtained, AnciCare will instruct the MRI center to send the films by two-day carrier, which will limit the cost of the program while providing reasonable, turn-around-time for review.   

Our computerized scheduling program is modified to diary each referral for second opinion and monitor the status of each request.  AnciCare will enter the data obtained into our computer system and run Quality Assurance reports twice daily to monitor the status of each second opinion requested and assure that it is being handled in an expedient manner.   

AnciCare will then inform our board-certified radiologists that a review is requested.  AnciCare will request that the second opinion be completed within two business days from receipt of the films.   

AnciCare will continue to monitor the status of the second opinion and will place calls to the radiologists to confirm that the films were received and the second opinion will be completed on time.   

The second opinion report will be faxed to AnciCare’s attention, which in turn will fax the report to your attention.  This will allow AnciCare to confirm that the second opinion was completed and is legible.   

AnciCare will select and provide for your company a panel of board certified Radiologists who have met AnciCare's credentialing criteria described herein, and who will handle all volume of second opinions requested by your company.  

The charge for this service is $200 per second opinion requested.  This fee includes the cost of the reading by the Board Certified radiologist, the mailing of the films, and the administrative costs of AnciCare’s coordination of the second opinion, quality assurance functions and report.
